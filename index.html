About Me

"To be is to be with." - Gabriel Marcel

Who am I? A lot of times we answer this question with different attributes of who we are. I am tall. I am short. I am a college graduate. Rarely that we define ourselves by the very people who are around us - the same people who shapes us. I am who I am because of my family. The very core of who I am and who I want to be is my family.

I am Alvin Ayusa. A husband to a loving wife, Joy. A loving father to two adorable kids, Julian and Aletheia. This is who I am. Defining my whole existence for my family and by my family.
